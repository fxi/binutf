Binary to UTF-16 explorer

Explore UTF-16 world by clicking on checkboxes. Yep. And use it to golf code or something...
  • Click on checkboxes to produce UTF-16 strings from binary
  • Checked = 1, unchecked = 0
  • The result is shown in the gray box
  • Empty lines are ignored (for now)
  • May produce weird or invisible characters
